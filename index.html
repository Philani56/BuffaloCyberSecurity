About Us

Buffalo Cyber Security, founded in 2022 by Bessy Mosomane, specializes in providing top-tier digital solutions to modern businesses. Our expertise covers cybersecurity, custom app development, and cloud migration, ensuring our clients stay competitive and secure in an evolving digital landscape.

We deliver comprehensive protection against cyber threats, including malware, phishing, and data breaches. Our cutting-edge defenses are tailored to each business’s needs, offering resilience and peace of mind.

Our custom-built mobile apps enhance digital presence and customer engagement, providing seamless and user-friendly experiences that drive brand loyalty and growth.

Additionally, we facilitate smooth cloud migrations to AWS, improving efficiency and scalability while minimizing disruption.

At Buffalo Cyber Security, we are committed to innovation and excellence, offering tailored solutions that drive growth, security, and sustainability. Trust us to be your partner in navigating the digital era.
